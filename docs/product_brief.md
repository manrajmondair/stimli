# Product Brief

## Customer

The first customer is a DTC growth team or founder running paid social tests with frequent creative fatigue and limited budget. The buyer wants faster pre-launch decisions, clearer creative edits, and a record of why each variant was shipped.

## Core Workflow

1. Add creative variants: script, landing page, static creative notes, audio transcript, or video transcript.
2. Add campaign context: brand, audience, category, offer, required claims, and forbidden terms.
3. Compare variants.
4. Review the recommended winner, score deltas, timeline evidence, and edit cards.
5. Draft a challenger focused on hook, offer, CTA, or clarity.
6. Export a report.
7. Log launch outcomes so the workspace can learn from real performance.

## Product Principles

- Comparison-first: the user should know what to ship.
- Action-first: every weakness should become a concrete edit.
- Multimodal-ready: the asset model supports text, landing pages, image notes, audio, and video.
- Provider-neutral: brain-response inference is isolated behind a provider boundary.
- Outcome-aware: logged launch results become the basis for calibration and future defensibility.

## Near-Term Roadmap

- Improve extraction quality with stronger OCR, transcription diarization, and landing-page screenshot capture.
- Expand saved brand profiles, reusable campaign briefs, imports, and team creative libraries into the daily workflow.
- Deepen outcome calibration so scoring weights can be tuned by category, channel, audience, placement, and creative format.
- Add ad-platform import/export for Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and creative libraries.
- Keep hardening enterprise controls: SSO/SAML, SCIM, audit exports, retention automation, role-based permissions, and admin analytics.

## Enterprise Controls Now In Product

- Team roles for owner, admin, analyst, and viewer workspaces.
- Audit events for key workspace actions.
- Hosted inference observability with job status and retry recovery.
- Governance policy surface, workspace export, and deletion-review requests.
- Brand profiles, creative library metadata, bulk imports, and built-in benchmark validation.
